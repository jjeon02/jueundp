Korean Gul-ja(Word)

Below are the structure of gul-ja, which is a unit that does not exist in English.

가

consonant + vowel


The most basic structure. This is the minimum structure that is required in order to make a word

각

consonant + vowel + consonant


Consonant sound different depend on their location.

갉

Consonant + vowel + consonant + consonant


The maximum number of consonants that can be at the end of gul-ja is

깕

Double consonant + vowel + consonant + consonant


Only double consonant can be located at the front of pronunciation.
